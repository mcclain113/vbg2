Ten to Eleven Digit NDC Converter (10 to 11 Digit NDC Converter)


This is a simple tool for converting a list of formatted 10 digit NDCs to the raw 11 digit NDC format

For now, I am sharing the original excel template I created years ago

Getting started:

  1. Download the sample MS Excel file: Barry's NDC Converter.
  2. Simply paste your NDCs into the 10 digit NDC column.
  3. Customize the spreadsheet to your liking.
